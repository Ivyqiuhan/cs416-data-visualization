This chart is to compare the total amount of each product line category.

  • The y-axis is the total price calculated by each product line category.
  • The x-axis is 6 product line category.
  • The color of each bar is determined by of the product line category.

This chart is to find the relationship between Unit Price of the item and Purchase Time and Gender.

  • The y-axis is the unit price of that purchase.
  • The x-axis is the time of that purchase.
  • The size of each bubble is the quantity of that purchase.
  • The color of each bubble is the gender of the person.

This chart is to show every sale data point in each month and colored by product line category.

  • The y-axis is the total price of that purchase.
  • The x-axis is the day of the month.
  • The color of each bubble is determined by of the product line category.
  • The slider is to filter by each month.
